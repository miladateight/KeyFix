KeyFix · AT8 project

Stop fighting the wrong keyboard layout

A privacy-first Windows tray app that catches wrong keyboard-layout typing, alerts you, and can correct the previous word the moment you press Space — without sending anything anywhere.

Windows 10 / 11 Local-only, no telemetry Latest release: v0.5.0
Download installer View on GitHub

The problem

Switching between English, Persian, Arabic, or German while typing makes it easy to forget which keyboard layout is active. You mean to type hello, but the layout is Persian, so the result is اثممخ — and you usually only notice after sending it.

What KeyFix does

  • Watches typing locally in a short in-memory buffer.
  • Checks the previous word against your enabled language profiles after Space.
  • Alerts, suggests, or automatically corrects and switches layout — your choice.
  • Never saves or uploads anything you type.

Features

What you get

Built around one rule: useful detection without ever sending your text anywhere.

Three detection modes

AlertOnly, AlertAndSuggest, and AutoSwitch — pick how much KeyFix should do for you.

Correction after Space

KeyFix fixes the previous word only after you press Space — it never rewrites a word while you're still typing it.

Per-language control

Enable only the layouts you actually use: English, Persian, Arabic, or German.

Exclusion list

Terminals, password managers, and other sensitive apps are excluded by default.

Local-only by design

No telemetry, no remote server. Settings live in %APPDATA%\KeyFix\settings.json.

Release-tested build

A compact settings panel, first-run setup flow, and a 43-test xUnit suite behind the release.

Detection modes

Choose how much KeyFix does

AlertOnly

Plays a sound and/or shows a notification when a likely wrong-layout word is detected.

AlertAndSuggest

Alerts you and suggests the keyboard layout that matches better.

AutoSwitch

Corrects the previous word after Space and switches to the suggested layout automatically.

How it works

From keystroke to correction

01

Type normally

KeyFix keeps a short in-memory buffer of your recent typing — nothing is written to disk.

02

Press Space

It checks the previous word against your enabled language profiles and keyboard layout maps.

03

Get the right result

Depending on your mode, KeyFix alerts you, suggests a layout, or corrects the word and switches input language.
